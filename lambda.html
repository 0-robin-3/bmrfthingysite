Lambda Complex (Sector F)

Welcome to the Lambda Complex, the heart of Black Mesa's research into advanced physics and quantum mechanics. Here, we explore the mysteries of the universe and push the boundaries of scientific knowledge.
Currently we are at the brink of creating a method of teleportation.

The Lambda Complex is the most advanced sector within Black Mesa, dedicated to experimental teleportation technology and high-energy physics. It houses the powerful Lambda Reactor, which is essential for inter-dimensional research and the development of teleportation devices. Access to the Lambda Complex is highly restricted, with only authorized personnel permitted beyond security checkpoints. The facility is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories, containment chambers, and a dedicated team of scientists and engineers working on top-secret projects.
